Example: A 30×40 ft plot = 111 m². FAR = 1.75. Max total floor area = 111 × 1.75 = 194 m² (2,090 sq ft) across all floors.
Key definitions
Setback
Mandatory open space between the building and plot boundary. Ensures ventilation, access and safety.
FAR / FSI
Floor Area Ratio = Total floor area ÷ Plot area. Controls density of development on a plot.
Ground Coverage
% of plot covered by building footprint at ground level. Limited to preserve open space.
Carpet Area
Usable floor area within walls, excluding wall thickness and balcony. ~85–90% of built-up area.

Parking provisions are mandatory and must be within the plot boundary. Basement parking is allowed and encouraged for plots above 200 m².

RWH system components
🏠
Rooftop Collection
All terraces must have outlets connected to the RWH system. Slope min. 1:100.
🚿
First Flush Diverter
First 2–3 mm of rain diverted before harvesting begins to remove pollutants.
💧
Storage Tank / Sump
Capacity: Roof area (m²) × 20–25 mm rainfall. Min. 5,000 litres for plots under 4,000 sq ft.
🌱
Percolation Pit
For plots > 4,000 sq ft. Pit size: 1m × 1m × 2m min. with gravel fill for groundwater recharge.
☀️ Solar water heating mandatory for plots above 600 m² (6,458 sq ft). Min. 1 m² panel area per 100 L of daily hot water requirement. BESCOM offers incentives.
